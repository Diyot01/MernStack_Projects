    History of INDIA

    Indian history encompasses the Indus Valley Civilization, Vedic Age, Mauryan Empire (led by Ashoka), Gupta Empire, Mughal Empire, and the British Raj, culminating in India's independence in 1947. Key developments include advancements in mathematics (like the concept of zero), philosophy, and religious diversity with the rise of Jainism and Buddhism. The modern era saw the growth of Indian nationalism, significant movements led by Mahatma Gandhi, and the establishment of the Indian Republic.
